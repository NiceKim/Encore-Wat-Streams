The Apsara Dance

Duration: 90 minutes
Language: Khmer with English subtitles
Next Show: June 15, 2024 | 7:00 PM

Experience the mesmerizing grace of the Apsara Dance, a classical Cambodian performance that traces its roots to the royal courts of ancient Angkor. This UNESCO-recognized masterpiece features intricate hand gestures, stunning costumes, and ethereal movements that tell stories of celestial beings.

About the Performance

  • Traditional royal court dance dating back to the 7th century
  • Performed by highly trained dancers in elaborate costumes
  • Features intricate hand movements and poses inspired by temple carvings
  • Accompanied by traditional Pinpeat orchestra
  • Includes storytelling through dance about mythological stories and legends

What to Expect

Immerse yourself in an evening of cultural excellence as our skilled performers bring to life the ancient art of Apsara Dance. The performance includes:

  • Welcome ceremony with traditional music
  • Main Apsara Dance performance
  • Interactive demonstration of hand gestures
  • Photo opportunity with performers (optional)
